Our Services

In Helping Better Understand Your Mental Health

Career Guidance Counselling
We provide career guidance which is a program intended to help people in making and implementing informed occupational and educational choices.
Behavioral Modification Counselling
We also provide behavior modification counselling were we try to alter behavioral patterns of an individual by using different learning techniques such as biofeedback.
Marriage Counseling
We also provide marriage counseling which aids couples to know and resolve conflicts within their relationship by helping them get the necessary tools to communicate and argue in a healthier way.
Financial Management Advice
We also provide financial management advice where we advise others on planning and execution of our advice to meet risk mitigation objectives and investment.
General Guidance
We also help and advice about how to deal with general problems such as problems connected to your work, education and personal relationships.
